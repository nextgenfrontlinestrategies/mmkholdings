Case Study: How MMK NovaTech Boosted Efficiency for Kagisho Logistics

Transforming Freight Operations with Custom Technology

The Challenge

Kagisho Logistics, a leading Kimberley-based freight company, faced mounting delays and errors due to manual tracking. Their growth demanded a smarter, scalable solution.

Our Solution

MMK NovaTech engineered a custom platform: automated fleet management, live GPS tracking, ERP integration, and a secure client portal. Delivered MVP in 10 weeks, iterated with real user feedback.

Impact in 6 Months

  • 40% reduction in delivery delays
  • Real-time tracking for clients via secure portal
  • Automated maintenance alerts, boosting fleet uptime by 25%
  • Advanced reporting for smarter decisions
“Our agile team worked closely with Kagisho’s staff, delivering a minimum viable product in just 10 weeks and iterating based on user feedback. The result? Improved customer satisfaction and stronger profitability.”
